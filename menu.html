Beverages


Creole Creamery

A couple scoops of your choice of any Creole Creamery flavor served in a frosty parfait glass, and pour over an old-school New Orleans Nectar Soda, with its almond-cherry flavor and bright pink color. A timeless taste of old New Orleans.

 

HiVolt

For recovering from the night before, for an afternoon lift, or just because… the Cola Cocktail, composed of two espresso shots, vanilla syrup, and Mexican coke, nearly defines the word “jolt.” Also, it’s mighty tasty.

 

Hurricane

It's a little bit sweet and a little bit tart, and comes in a pretty glass. Invented in New Orleans, there's only one Hurricane. Dark rum, passion fruit syrup, fresh lemon juice or lime juice, garnished with orange slice and a cherry.
